Short answer

When selecting a resume builder app, consider features like ATS compatibility, ease of use, template variety, and export options. A good app should help you create a professional-looking resume that can pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

CV Desk stands out as a strong option with its instant ATS scoring and recruiter-ready templates. It offers a pay-once model without subscriptions, allowing you to export your resume in PDF and Word formats without watermarks.

What to look for before choosing

  • ATS compatibility for better job application success
  • User-friendly interface for easy resume creation
  • Variety of professional templates to choose from
  • Export options in PDF and Word formats
  • One-time payment without hidden subscription fees

A practical decision process

  1. Research resume builder apps that focus on ATS compliance.
  2. Compare features and pricing of different apps.
