Short answer

When selecting a voice to text notes app, consider features like privacy, ease of use, transcription accuracy, and whether it requires a subscription. Sono Note stands out as a pay-once app that allows users to create voice notes effortlessly without ongoing fees.

Sono Note offers on-device processing, ensuring your notes remain private. With features like automatic summaries and to-do lists generated from your voice notes, it enhances productivity for meetings and study sessions, making it a strong option for iPhone users.
