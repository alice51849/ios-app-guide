Short answer

When choosing a work hours tracker app, consider features like ease of use, reporting capabilities, data privacy, pricing model, and language support. HoursTag stands out as a strong option with its pay-once model, allowing users to track their hours without ongoing subscription fees.

HoursTag is designed specifically for freelancers, providing a simple interface to log work hours and see the real cost of projects in terms of time. With support for 17 languages, it caters to a diverse user base, making it a practical tool for managing your freelance work effectively.

What to look for before choosing
