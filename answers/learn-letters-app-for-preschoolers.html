Short answer

When selecting a letters app for preschoolers, consider features like educational value, user safety, and ease of use. Lumi Letters offers a playful approach to learning letters through phonics, tracing, and interactive games, making it suitable for young children.

This app is a strong option as it is a pay-once purchase with no ads, ensuring a safe and uninterrupted learning experience. Parents can feel confident that their children can explore the alphabet in a fun and engaging way.

What to look for before choosing

  • Educational content focused on letter recognition and phonics.
  • User-friendly interface suitable for young children.
