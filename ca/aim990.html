What is Aim990?

Aim990 is an iOS app. Master TOEIC Listening & Reading

Unlock your potential with Aim990, the ultimate TOEIC(R) Listening & Reading test preparation app. Designed for learners aiming for a top score of 990, Aim990 offers a comprehensive 30-day study program tailored just for you.

Each day, you'll receive a carefully crafted study plan that combines listening and reading exercises to ensure balanced skill development. Our weak-spot drills help you focus on areas that need improvement, allowing you to maximize your study efficiency.

Experience the thrill of timed practice sessions that simulate real exam conditions. This feature not only prepares you for the pressure of the test but also improves your time management skills. After each practice, you can review your mistakes and track your progress over time with our intuitive score tracking system.

What sets Aim990 apart? We offer a one-time unlock option so you can enjoy full access without the hassle of subscriptions. Plus, we respect your privacy—there are no ads, and you don’t need to create an account. All your progress is stored securely on your device, allowing you to study offline whenever and wherever you like.

With Aim990, you’ll have the tools you need to enhance your TOEIC preparation journey. Join countless learners who are on the path to success, and let’s work together to achieve that coveted score of 990!
