What is Aim990?

Aim990 is an iOS app. Master TOEIC Listening & Reading

Achieve your TOEIC goals with Aim990, your dedicated study companion for mastering the Listening and Reading sections of the exam. With our 30-day structured study plan, you’ll engage in daily lessons tailored to help you improve and reach that coveted score of 990.

Each day, you’ll tackle focused study sessions that include weak-spot drills designed to target your specific areas for improvement. Our timed practice tests simulate real exam conditions, helping you build the confidence and speed needed to excel. After each practice session, you’ll receive a comprehensive mistake review, allowing you to learn from your errors and track your progress effectively.

Aim990 is designed for convenience. With a one-time unlock option, you can access all features without the hassle of subscriptions. Enjoy an ad-free experience that respects your time and focus. Plus, there’s no need to create an account, ensuring your progress remains private and secure on your device.

Whether you’re studying at home or on the go, Aim990 works offline, giving you the flexibility to prepare whenever and wherever suits you best. Our user-friendly interface is localized in 50 languages, making it accessible for learners from diverse backgrounds.

Join countless others who are ready to elevate their TOEIC scores. Download Aim990 today and take the first step toward achieving your language goals. Remember, Aim990 is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with ETS; TOEIC is a trademark of ETS.
