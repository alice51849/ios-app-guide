What is G+Money?

G+Money is an iOS app. Budget & Travel Expenses

G+Money keeps everyday money tracking simple and friendly. Log spending by category, trip or event, set a daily budget and see where your money goes without wrestling with a heavy finance app.

For travel, the built-in currency converter is fast and works with live or manual rates, even offline. For daily life, filters, totals and CSV export make it easy to understand habits and keep records.

No account, no ads and no tracking. Your financial data stays on your device. Clean design, one-time purchase, never a subscription.
