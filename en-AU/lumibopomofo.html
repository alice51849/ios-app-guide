What is Lumi Bopomofo?

Lumi Bopomofo is an iOS app. Mandarin for kids

A cheerful way for little learners to meet Mandarin sounds.

Lumi Bopomofo introduces Zhuyin, the Mandarin Chinese phonetic system, through a cosy planet adventure made for children aged 3–6. Kids practise matching symbols, sounds and combinations in short, friendly challenges.

• Explore 注音 / Zhuyin through play

• Cute space friends and calm feedback

• Great for home learning, preschool and bilingual families

• Offline play, no ads

• One-time purchase with in-app unlock

Built for parents who want a gentle start to Chinese phonics.
