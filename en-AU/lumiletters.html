What is Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids?

Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids is an iOS app. Letter sounds & tracing

A calm and cute way to learn the English ABC. Lumi Letters Lite supports children aged 3–6 with planet-themed games, clear sounds and friendly tracing practice for kindy, preschool and learning at home.
