What is Snapport: Passport Photos?

Create passport, visa and ID photos at home — the calm, private way. Snapport gives you common size templates, face-alignment guides, background tools and print-ready layouts, all processed right on your iPhone.

PAY ONCE. NO SUBSCRIPTION.

Buy Snapport once and it’s yours. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads, no tracking, no account.

CHOOSE A SIZE

• Common passport, visa, ID, student card, résumé and work-badge templates for many regions

• Includes popular sizes such as US 2×2, UK/Schengen/Japan/Korea/Australia 35×45 mm, Canada 50×70 mm, China 33×48 mm, India 2×2 and more

• Create custom sizes in mm, inches or px, with any DPI and any background

ALIGN WITH CONFIDENCE

• On-device Apple Vision face detection with head-top, eye-line and chin guides

• Pinch, drag and rotate; auto-centre the face or reset anytime

BACKGROUND & TOUCH-UPS

• Replace the background with white, grey, blue or red — or keep the original

• On-device person segmentation and natural adjustments for brightness, contrast, warmth, exposure and sharpness

EXPORT & PRINT

• Export JPEG, PNG or PDF

• Print sheets for 4×6, A4 or Letter with multiple copies and cut guides

• Save to Photos or Files, or share

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• Everything is processed on your device — your photos are never uploaded

• No account, no ads, no tracking, no cloud

• Works fully offline except for the optional template update check

IMPORTANT

Photo requirements vary by country, agency and application type. Snapport provides common templates and guides; it does not guarantee acceptance and is not affiliated with any government. Always review the official requirements before submitting.
