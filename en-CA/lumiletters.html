What is Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids?

Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids is an iOS app. ABC Tracing & Phonics Games

A warm first alphabet game for young learners. Lumi Letters Lite helps children aged 3–6 practise English ABC skills with cute planets, clear pronunciation and gentle activities for preschool, kindergarten and home learning.

• Trace uppercase and lowercase letters with correct stroke order

• Tap to hear letter sounds and phonics pronunciation

• Planet challenge levels support focus and memory

• Montessori-style learning for parents and children

• No ads, works offline and feels safe for families

Start learning letters in a calm, playful way.
