What is PicClear Pro?

PicClear Pro is an iOS app. Clean photos, free up space

Free up gigabytes by clearing out the photos you don’t need: duplicates, screenshots, blurry shots and space-hungry videos.

PicClear Pro scans your photo library on your device, groups items that are safe to review and lets you check everything before a single photo is deleted. Your photos are never uploaded, there’s no account to create and there are no ads.

Scan and preview for free. When you’re ready to clean, one simple one-time unlock enables deleting forever — no subscription.

Review thumbnails, protect Favourites and keep the suggested best shot in each group. A private, calm way to reclaim storage and keep your library under control.
