What is Sono Note?

Sono Note is an iOS app. Voice notes, summaries & tasks

Speak naturally. Walk away with writing you're proud of.

Sono Note turns the messy way you actually think — out loud — into clean, structured writing in seconds. Capture a thought, a lecture, a client call, or a quick update, and Sono Note transcribes it, tidies it up, and hands you a finished note: a clear summary, the key points, your action items, and a follow-up email ready to send. Often before you've even put your phone down.

This isn't a meeting bot or a plain recorder. It's a private writing studio that turns your voice into work you can actually use.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT

• Speak for seconds — get a polished note, not a wall of raw text

• A crisp summary and key points, so you never have to listen again

• Action items pulled out for you as a ready-to-check list

• A follow-up email, drafted in the exact tone you choose

• Replay the original recording any time, right inside the note

• Export as text, Markdown, or PDF and share it anywhere

100% PRIVATE — BY DESIGN

Everything happens on your iPhone. No account. No ads. No tracking. No cloud. Your voice and your notes never leave your device, and the app works exactly the same in airplane mode. Privacy isn't a setting here — it's the whole architecture.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS

Back up everything to a single dated file and restore it in one tap. Browse, replay, share the original audio, or delete every recording from one simple place. You're always in control.

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE

Ready-made templates for work updates, client calls, classes, interviews, doctor visits, ideas, support cases, and project recaps get you a genuinely useful result on the very first try — in dozens of languages you can switch any time.

START FREE, GO UNLIMITED

Start free with 3 notes a day, up to 3 minutes each. Go Pro with a single one-time purchase — no subscription, ever — for unlimited notes, recordings up to 3 hours, PDF export, advanced templates, custom vocabulary, every follow-up email tone, and full backup & restore.

Your best thinking happens out loud. Sono Note makes sure none of it slips away.
