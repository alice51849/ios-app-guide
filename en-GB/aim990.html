What is Aim990?

Aim990 is an iOS app. Boost Your TOEIC Score!

Aim990 is your ultimate partner in conquering the TOEIC Listening & Reading test. Designed with a 30-day structured study plan, our app provides daily Listening and Reading exercises tailored to boost your skills step by step.

Each day, you'll engage in focused weak-spot drills that target your specific areas for improvement, ensuring you make the most of your study time. With our timed practice sessions, you'll build both speed and confidence, preparing you for the real test environment. After each practice, review your mistakes with detailed insights that help you learn and grow.

Track your progress effortlessly as you work toward the coveted TOEIC score of 990. Our user-friendly interface allows you to see how far you've come, keeping you motivated throughout your journey.

What sets Aim990 apart? We offer a one-time unlock option, allowing you to access all features without a subscription. Enjoy a completely ad-free experience, ensuring you can focus on your studies without distractions. Plus, your progress is kept private on your device, and you can study offline anytime, anywhere.

With our app localized in 50 languages, we cater to learners from diverse backgrounds, making it easier than ever to enhance your English skills.
