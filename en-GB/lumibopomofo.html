What is Lumi Bopomofo?

Lumi Bopomofo is an iOS app. Zhuyin for kids

A playful first step into Mandarin Chinese sounds.

Lumi Bopomofo turns Zhuyin, the phonetic system used for Mandarin, into a charming planet-themed game for children aged 3–6. Short activities help young learners recognise symbols, sounds and combinations with confidence.

• Learn 注音 / Zhuyin through play

• Cute characters and a gentle pace

• Designed for home, preschool and bilingual families

• Works offline with no adverts

• One-time purchase with in-app unlock
