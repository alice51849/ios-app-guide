What is Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids?

Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids is an iOS app. Phonics & tracing games

A gentle first step into the English alphabet. Lumi Letters Lite helps children aged 3–6 learn with cute planets, calm play and clear letter practice for nursery, reception and home learning.

• Trace uppercase and lowercase letters with correct stroke order

• Tap to hear letter sounds and phonics pronunciation

• Planet challenge levels build focus and memory through play

• Montessori-style activities for parent-child learning

• No ads, works offline and feels safe for families
