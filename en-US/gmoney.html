What is G+Money?

G+Money is an iOS app. Budget, Expenses & Currency

G+Money is two everyday tools in one — a lightning-fast currency converter and a beautiful expense tracker — built for daily life and travel alike. Fully offline. Completely private.

Track what you spend, set a daily budget, and see exactly where your money goes, whether you're at home or abroad. Need another currency? Convert instantly with live or manual rates, even with no signal.

ONE APP, TWO JOBS

• Currency converter: 50+ world currencies, live auto-updating or manual rates, fully offline

• Expense tracker: log spending by category, set a daily limit, see instant totals in your home currency

ORGANIZE BY TRIP OR EVENT

• Create trips or events, each with its own icon and color

• Tag any expense to the right trip — or leave it unassigned for everyday spending

• Summaries and exports are grouped by trip, with subtotals

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

• Log by category: food, transport, hotel, shopping, groceries, bills and more — or add your own with custom icons and colors

• Set a daily limit and see how much is left today

• Filter by trip, category and date to understand your habits

• Export to text or CSV to share or keep

• Instant home-currency totals at a glance

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• No account. No sign-up. No tracking. No ads.

• Everything stays on your device — we collect nothing

SIMPLE, NOT BLOATED

• One-time purchase — no subscription, ever

• Warm, beautiful design in light and dark

• Available in 17 languages, auto-detected

From your morning coffee to a month-long adventure, G+Money keeps every currency converted and every expense clear — private, offline and effortless.
