What is Lumi Bopomofo?

Lumi Bopomofo is an iOS app. Learn Zhuyin the fun way

Lumi Bopomofo is a gentle, playful way for young children to learn the Bopomofo (Zhuyin) phonetic system.

Learn by playing

1. Feed the Friends - listen and pick the right symbol

2. Magic Tracing - trace each stroke with your finger

3. Tone Coaster - watch the cart ride to learn the four tones

4. Sound Train - blend initial + medial + final into a syllable

Collect & achieve

1. 37 adorable Bopomofo friends to collect

2. Earn stars and achievement badges

3. Hidden characters to discover!

Parent-friendly

1. Completely ad-free

2. Collects no personal data, no sign-up

3. Progress stored only on the device

4. English & Chinese interface

Help your child take a joyful first step into Bopomofo on Lumi's planet!
