What is Lumi Bopomofo Pro: Zhuyin?

Lumi Bopomofo Pro: Zhuyin is an iOS app. Zhuyin Phonics Game for Kids

Help your child fall in love with Chinese phonics through one adorable space adventure.

Lumi Bopomofo Pro is the complete edition of our Zhuyin and Bopomofo learning game, made for children ages 3 to 7. It turns the 37 Bopomofo sounds into a playful cosmic journey, so kids learn pronunciation, blending and tones naturally as they play. This helps build a strong foundation for preschool and first grade Mandarin.

4. Pay once, play forever, and share with the whole family.

WHAT YOUR CHILD LEARNS

1. Recognize and read all 37 Bopomofo symbols, from the first sound to full blending.

2. Blending. Combine initials, medials and finals into a complete sound.

3. Tones. Learn to tell the five tones apart through interactive practice.

4. Stroke order and symbol shapes. Trace with a finger to write neatly.

FIVE INTERACTIVE GAMES

1. Match and Feed. Feed cute companions the right sound.

2. Magic Tracing. Follow the strokes and master the shapes.

3. Tone Roller Coaster. Hear and learn the tones in motion.

4. Bopomofo Fishing. Listen to the blend and catch the right fish.

5. Star Race. Beat the clock and your own best score.

The app also includes collectible companions, daily goals, achievement badges and weak spot review, so kids want to come back every day.

PEACE OF MIND FOR PARENTS
