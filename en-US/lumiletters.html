What is Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids?

Lumi Letters Lite: ABC Kids is an iOS app. ABC tracing & letter games

Download free, play first, and unlock only if your child loves it.

Lumi Letters Lite turns the alphabet into a dreamy starry adventure. Your child is not sitting through a lesson. They are playing a game they will not want to put down. They light up planets, trace beautiful letters, listen for sounds, and collect twenty six adorable letter friends. Every screen, every voice line and every melody has been polished for children aged three to seven.

Everything in the free version

All activities for the letters A to D are fully open. Children start by hearing each letter name and sound, then trace uppercase and lowercase letters with real stroke order and crosshair guide lines that lead little hands to neat handwriting.

A cute listen and tap challenge links each sound to its shape.

The first two planet adventures are fully playable. Clear a level and your child builds a planet of their very own.

The first three game types on the achievements page show you how your child is doing.

One purchase unlocks the whole universe

All twenty six letters, uppercase and lowercase, to play and to trace.

Fourteen planet adventures with twelve game types that grow step by step. Listening for letters, first sounds, ending sounds, middle vowels, matching uppercase to lowercase, filling in missing letters and letter order. Without noticing it, your child builds a complete foundation for early reading.

Daily challenges, timed races and free practice keep every day fresh.

A learning dashboard breaks performance into four skills, letter recognition, letter order, sound awareness and picture reading, so strengths and gaps are clear at a glance.

Export a PDF worksheet with one tap. It automatically includes the letters your child practiced today, with tracing grids ready to print, so learning continues beyond the screen.

Collect all twenty six letter friends and earn the final champion badge.
