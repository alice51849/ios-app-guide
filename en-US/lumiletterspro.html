Welcome to Lumi Letters

In a magical forest, kids team up with Lumi the bear to take all 26 letters from "I know it" to "I can read, write and sound it out." This isn't another flashcard app. It's a complete phonics journey, carefully sequenced to give young learners the decoding skills that last a lifetime.

Why parents choose Lumi

Real phonics, not just the ABC song: first the letter NAME and letter SOUND, then beginning, ending and middle sounds, then picture-to-letter reading, the same path used in English-speaking kindergartens.

Progress you can see: the Parent Zone breaks every session into four core skills (Letter Recognition, Alphabet Order, Phonemic Awareness, Picture Phonics) so you know exactly what is strong and what needs more practice.

Practice you can take with you: export a PDF "Today's Worksheet" with the exact letters your child practiced, big trace boxes and crosshair guides. Print it and practice off-screen, too.

What kids get to play

ABC Land: all 26 letters, uppercase and lowercase tracing. Real stroke shapes and crosshair guide lines build a steady pencil grip and beautiful handwriting.

Letter Challenge: listen and tap the right letter with playful, colorful bubbles, locking the SOUND to the SHAPE.

Planet Adventure: 14 planets and 12 question types, including listen, picture first or last sound, upper and lowercase match, missing letter, and what comes next or before, gently ramping up as they grow.

26 Letter Friends to collect: unlocked only by finishing tracing and the challenge, the perfect "just one more" motivation.

Daily Challenge and Time Race: a fresh set every day builds a habit, and beating the race unlocks a special friend.
