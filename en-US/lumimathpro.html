What is Lumi Math Pro: Kids Numbers?

Lumi Math Pro: Kids Numbers is an iOS app. Competition-style brain math

Turn competition-level math thinking into a space adventure kids can't put down

Hop aboard Lumi the bear's rocket and journey across 14 breathtaking, movie-quality planets. Every correct answer lights up a planet and earns a space friend — building world-class math thinking, one fun puzzle at a time.

BUILT ON COMPETITION-STYLE QUESTION TYPES — 46 OF THEM

Inspired by the early-grade style of the WMI (World Mathematics Invitational) and similar international contests: number patterns, logic & reasoning, sequences, comparison, ordinals, combinations, story problems and geometry — plus a rock-solid core of addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, place value and carrying. Not random questions — a design that genuinely trains mathematical thinking.

(This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to any competition organizer; question types are a style reference only.)

NOT JUST FOR KIDS — TRAIN YOUR BRAIN TOO

These puzzles test observation, reasoning and logic. Perfect for ages 5–8, great for older students, and a real brain workout for grown-ups. Play together as a family and get sharper together.

PLAYABLE BEFORE THEY CAN READ

Questions use cute clay-art examples you understand at a glance, with full voice guidance (slow-speech option for little ones) — so even pre-readers can adventure on their own.

WEAK-SPOT PRACTICE — DRILL EXACTLY WHAT YOU MISS

The app tracks how you do on every question type. Practice mode gathers your most-missed topics, swaps in fresh numbers and drills them — real reinforcement, not busywork.

FIVE SKILLS, TRACKED

Number & Operations · Patterns & Sequences · Logic & Reasoning · Geometry & Space · Real-life Application. The Progress tab shows completion and a score for each — so you always know where you shine and where to push.

EPIC, IMMERSIVE VISUALS

Deep-space galaxies, glowing planets, cinematic effects and music — console-game quality that pulls you in from the very first tap.

A REASON TO OPEN IT EVERY DAY

• Daily Challenge: a fresh set each day, with a growing streak

• Time Race: 60 seconds for a high score — unlock a cool special friend

• Badges & Friends: 12 achievements and 14 space friends to collect

PRINTABLE WORKSHEETS & LEARNING REPORTS

In the Parent Zone, generate a 20-question worksheet at your child's current level (with answer key) and save it as a PDF or print it — screen-free practice anywhere. Export a one-page Learning Report PDF too, so parents and teachers can see every skill and weak spot at a glance.
