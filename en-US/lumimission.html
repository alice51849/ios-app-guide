What is Lumi Mission Planet: Routines?

Lumi Mission Planet: Routines is an iOS app. Reward Chart & Kids Routines

Getting a small child to brush teeth, finish meals or tidy toys should not take a battle every single day.

Lumi Mission Planet turns daily routines into gentle missions. Your child taps the screen for every small win, and an adorable bunny buddy jumps, cheers and grows with them. Finish a mission and the whole planet celebrates.

Made for parents of children aged about three to seven, and designed to be used together.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

Every single bite, every brushed tooth, every toy back in the basket gets an instant, joyful reaction. Children stay motivated because the feedback is immediate, not a sticker at the end of the week.

KEY FEATURES

Daily missions: meals, tooth brushing, sleep time, tidy-up and school prep

Create your own custom mission with a background your child loves

Six buddy characters to collect: bunny, bear, kitten, panda, dinosaur and fox

One hundred achievement stickers across missions, streaks, perfect days and play

A buddy that greets your child by nickname and grows day after day

Beautiful victory cards you can save and share with family

Parent dashboard with a glance view of today, seven day calendar and full history

Per-tap log with the exact time of every completion, exportable as CSV

Daily reminder at a time that fits your family

Backup export and import so progress moves safely to a new phone

Available in English, Traditional Chinese, Japanese and Korean

PRIVACY FIRST

No account, no ads, no third party analytics, no tracking. Everything stays on your device. The optional nickname is only used inside the app and never leaves the phone.

ONE-TIME PURCHASE

Meals, tooth brushing, sleep and tidy-up missions are free, together with the bunny buddy and the first stickers in every category. A single one-time purchase unlocks the school prep mission, custom missions, all six buddies and the full sticker collection forever. No subscription.

Bedtime, mealtime and tidy-up can feel like wins for everyone. Let Lumi cheer your child on.
