What is Lumi Weather: Family Forecast?

Lumi Weather: Family Forecast is an iOS app. Outfit & best time to go out

Some mornings you stand at the window with a wriggling toddler and one quiet worry: is it too cold, too hot, too wet to take them out today?

Lumi Weather was made for that exact moment.

It turns a wall of numbers into a single, gentle answer. One look tells you whether today is your child's day to run in the park or a cozy day to stay in, and a warm little character named Lumi is right there with you, dressed for whatever the sky is doing.

A score you can trust in a glance

Lumi blends feels-like temperature, UV, rain chance and wind, then weighs it for how sensitive your child's age really is. You get a clear outing score and a kind, plain-language verdict. Tap once and every point is explained. No mystery, no hidden math, just honesty you can feel good about.

The moment that makes parents smile

Lumi finds the best window of the day to head outside, so you never miss the gentle hours. It hands you an outfit checklist that quietly changes with the weather and your child's age, then offers more than a hundred play ideas for sun, rain and everything between, fresh every single day.

A little world that feels alive

Sunrise glows, rain taps softly, snow drifts slow and big, and at night the sky goes quiet and starry. Lumi puts on a sun hat, hides under an umbrella, or bundles up in a scarf, so even a child who cannot read yet understands the day at a glance. It is the kind of detail you usually only feel in apps that cost far more.

Made for the people who care most

Lumi Weather is for parents, guardians and caregivers. The smaller the child, the more gently it advises. It respects your morning, your time and your peace of mind.

Free to start, one unlock, yours forever

The full Today experience is free every day: the outing score, dress-up Lumi, tips and reminders. When you love it, a single unlock gives you the hourly and weekly forecasts, the get-ready checklist, multiple places and the Home Screen widget, forever. No subscriptions, no advertising, no third-party tracking, no account, and family sharing is included. Everything stays on your device. Location is used only to fetch local weather and is never uploaded or sold. Weather data is provided by Apple WeatherKit.

Available in English, Traditional Chinese, Japanese and Korean, following your system language or your own choice.

Lumi's suggestions are warm, careful guidance for families, not a guarantee of actual conditions. Always trust your own eyes on your own child.
