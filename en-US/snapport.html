What is Snapport: Passport & ID Photos?

Snapport: Passport & ID Photos is an iOS app. Visa, Prints & Backgrounds

Create passport, visa, and ID photos at home — the calm, private way. Snapport gives you common size templates, face-alignment guides, background tools, and print-ready layouts, all processed right on your iPhone.

PAY ONCE. NO SUBSCRIPTION.

Buy Snapport once and it’s yours. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads, no tracking, no account.

CHOOSE A SIZE

• Common passport, visa, ID, student ID, resume, and work-badge templates for many regions

• Includes popular sizes such as US 2×2, UK/Schengen/Japan/Korea/Australia 35×45mm, Canada 50×70mm, China 33×48mm, India 2×2, and more

• Create custom sizes (mm / inch / px, any DPI, any background)

• Templates are kept current on a 2026 standards baseline, with an optional offline-friendly “Check for Template Updates” that downloads only a public JSON size list and sends no personal data

ALIGN WITH CONFIDENCE

• On-device Apple Vision face detection with head-top, eye-line, and chin guides

• Pinch, drag, and rotate; auto-center the face; reset anytime

BACKGROUND & TOUCH-UPS

• Replace the background with white, gray, blue, red — or keep the original

• On-device person segmentation; review before and after

• Light, identity-safe adjustments (brightness, contrast, warmth, exposure, sharpen)

EXPORT & PRINT

• Export JPEG, PNG, or PDF

• Print sheets for 4×6, A4, or Letter with multiple copies and cut guides

• Save to Photos or Files, or share

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• Everything is processed on your device — your photos are never uploaded

• No account, no ads, no tracking, no cloud

• Works fully offline; the only network use is the optional, user-initiated template update check

IMPORTANT

Photo requirements vary by country, agency, and application type. Snapport provides common templates and guides — it does not guarantee acceptance and is not affiliated with any government. Always review the official requirements before submitting.
