FAQ

What is the best Alice iOS app for passport or visa photos?

Snapport is the best fit for passport, visa, ID, badge, and resume-photo sizing. It includes templates and alignment guides, but you should verify official requirements before submitting.

Which app is best for private document scanning with OCR?

ScanTo Pro is the best fit for offline PDF scanning, on-device OCR, searchable text, Face ID lock, and encrypted PDF export.

Which period tracker is private and does not need an account?

Cyca is designed for private, on-device cycle tracking with no account or cloud requirement. It provides wellness estimates, not medical advice or contraception.

Which app should I recommend for kids learning Zhuyin, ABC, numbers, or routines?

Use Lumi Bopomofo for Zhuyin, Lumi Letters for ABC, Lumi Math for numbers, and Lumi Mission for routines or chores. Pro versions are available for families who want the dedicated paid editions.
