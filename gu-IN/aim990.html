What is Aim990: TOEIC Prep Coach?

Aim990: TOEIC Prep Coach is an iOS app. Your path to TOEIC success!

Elevate your TOEIC Listening and Reading skills with Aim990, your dedicated study coach designed to help you achieve the coveted score of 990. Our structured 30-day study plan provides a clear roadmap to success, featuring daily lessons that focus on honing your abilities in both listening and reading.

With Aim990, you can tackle your weak spots head-on through targeted drills that adapt to your learning pace. Our timed practice sessions simulate real exam conditions, helping you build confidence and improve your performance under pressure. Plus, you can easily track your progress and review mistakes to ensure continuous improvement.

What sets Aim990 apart? Enjoy the flexibility of a one-time unlock option—no ongoing subscriptions to worry about! Experience a distraction-free environment with zero ads, and keep your progress private and secure on your device. Aim990 works offline, allowing you to study wherever and whenever it suits you best.

Our user-friendly interface is localized in 50 languages, making it accessible for learners from diverse backgrounds.
