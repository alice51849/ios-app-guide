HoursTag: A Unique Finance App for iPhone Users

Many people struggle to understand the true cost of their purchases, often viewing expenses solely in monetary terms. HoursTag offers a fresh perspective by translating prices into the hours of work required to earn that money, making it ideal for anyone looking to improve their financial mindset and budgeting habits.

  • Tagging feature to categorize spending as Need, Want, or Impulse.
  • Insights and analytics to track spending patterns over time.
  • No ongoing subscription fees, ensuring a one-time purchase.

HoursTag stands out by providing a unique approach to personal finance, allowing users to visualize their spending in terms of time rather than money.
