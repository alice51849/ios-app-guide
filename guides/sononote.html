What to look for

  • Privacy: Ensure the app processes data on-device without sending it to the cloud.
  • No subscriptions: Look for apps that offer a one-time purchase model.
  • Voice-to-text accuracy: The app should transcribe your voice clearly and accurately.
  • Summarization features: It should provide concise summaries and key points from your voice notes.
  • User-friendly interface: The app should be easy to navigate and use for quick note-taking.
