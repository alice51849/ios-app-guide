What is Aim990?

Aim990 is an iOS app. TOEIC Prep Made Easy

Prepare for the TOEIC like never before with Aim990! Our app offers a structured 30-day study plan that focuses on enhancing your listening and reading skills. Each day is designed to help you tackle various aspects of the exam, ensuring you cover all necessary topics and strategies.

With our weak-spot drills, you can identify and improve on your specific weaknesses, making your study time more effective. Engage in timed practice sessions that simulate real exam conditions, allowing you to build confidence and improve your time management skills.

Track your progress effortlessly with our integrated score tracking feature. By monitoring your improvements, you can adjust your study habits and focus areas to maximize your results as you work toward that coveted score of 990.

Aim990 is designed with user convenience in mind. Enjoy a one-time unlock option, with no ads and no account required. Your progress is kept private and secure on your device, and the app functions offline, so you can study anytime, anywhere.

With an interface localized in 50 languages, Aim990 is accessible to everyone, making TOEIC preparation straightforward and enjoyable.
