FAQ

Are my images uploaded?

No. Conversion happens client-side in your browser with canvas and a tiny built-in PDF writer.

Will the PDF be searchable?

No. This tool embeds images into a PDF. For searchable PDFs, use OCR in a scanner app such as ScanTo Pro and verify OCR behavior on your device.

What image order is used?
