What is Aim990?

Aim990 is an iOS app. TOEIC Prep Made Easy

Aim990 is your ultimate companion for conquering the TOEIC Listening & Reading test. Designed with a clear focus, our app offers a structured 30-day study plan that adapts to your needs, helping you maximize your study time and achieve your target score of 990.

With Aim990, you'll dive into daily Listening and Reading exercises tailored to enhance your skills. Our innovative weak-spot drills ensure that you focus on areas that need improvement, while timed practice sessions mimic the real exam experience, boosting your confidence and speed.

Track your progress effortlessly with our built-in score tracking feature. Monitor your improvements over time and stay motivated as you see your scores rise!

One of the standout features of Aim990 is the flexible one-time unlock option, allowing you to access all premium content without the hassle of a subscription. Enjoy an ad-free experience that keeps you focused on your studies, and rest assured that your data is safe and private, as everything is stored on your device and accessible offline.

Our user-friendly interface supports 50 languages, making it easy for learners from various backgrounds to navigate and utilize the app effectively. Aim990 is here to empower you on your journey to TOEIC success!
