Apakah Zodira?

Zodira ialah aplikasi iOS. AI astrology, tarot & horoscope

Zodira is an East-West AI astrology and tarot app for iPhone. It brings together horoscope, birth-chart insight, BaZi, Zi Wei and tarot-style reflection in one calm private experience.

It is designed for people who want personal guidance without accounts, ads or recurring subscriptions. Zodira works fully offline, keeps readings on your device, and does not track you.
