What is Aim990: TOEIC Prep Coach?

Aim990: TOEIC Prep Coach is an iOS app. Your path to TOEIC success!

Unlock your potential with Aim990, the ultimate TOEIC(R) Listening & Reading test prep app designed to help you ace the exam. Our 30-day study coach offers a structured approach to mastering the skills you need to reach your goal of a top score of 990.

With daily study plans tailored to your needs, you’ll engage in focused Listening and Reading practice that targets your weak spots. Our unique weak-spot drills ensure that you're not just practicing, but truly improving where it counts. Plus, with timed practice sessions, you’ll get accustomed to the exam format and pacing, helping you to perform at your best on test day.

Track your progress with our intuitive score tracking feature, allowing you to see how far you’ve come and where you need to focus your efforts. Aim990 is designed for convenience and flexibility. Enjoy a one-time unlock option. Plus, there are no ads to disrupt your learning experience.

Your study journey is private and secure, as all progress is stored on your device and accessible offline, making it perfect for studying on the go. With an interface localized in 50 languages, Aim990 caters to learners from diverse backgrounds.
