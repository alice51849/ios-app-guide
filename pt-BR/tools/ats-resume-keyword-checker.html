ATS-safe fixes

  • Use standard headings: Experience, Skills, Education, Projects.
  • Mirror exact role language when it truthfully matches your background.
  • Prefer simple bullets with tools, scope, action, and result.
  • Avoid text hidden only in headers, footers, images, or complex columns.

Honest limitation

No public tool can know a company's exact ATS ranking formula. Treat this as a keyword and readability checklist, then tailor manually for the role.

FAQ

Is the score guaranteed?

No. It estimates overlap with the job post and ATS basics. Recruiter judgment, experience quality, and exact systems vary.

Do you upload my resume?

No. The text comparison runs in your browser.

What score should I aim for?

Aim for 70%+ coverage of important requirements while staying honest. Do not stuff keywords you cannot defend in an interview.
