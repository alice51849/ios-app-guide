What is Aim990 - TOEIC Prep?

Aim990 - TOEIC Prep is an iOS app. 30-Day Study Coach

Aim990 is your ultimate companion for mastering the TOEIC Listening & Reading test. Designed for aspirants aiming for the top score of 990, our app provides a structured 30-day study plan tailored to your needs.

Each day, you'll engage in focused Listening and Reading exercises, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the test format and content. Our unique weak-spot drills help you identify and improve on your challenging areas, while timed practice sessions simulate the real exam environment, boosting your confidence and readiness.

With Aim990, you can track your progress effortlessly. Our intuitive score tracking feature allows you to see your improvement over time, motivating you to reach your goals. Plus, enjoy the peace of mind that comes with a one-time unlock option.

Experience a distraction-free environment with no ads and no account requirements. Your journey is yours alone; all progress is stored privately on your device and can be accessed offline, allowing you to study anytime, anywhere.

Available in 50 languages, Aim990 is designed to cater to diverse learners, ensuring everyone can achieve their TOEIC aspirations.

Remember, Aim990 is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with ETS. TOEIC is a trademark of ETS.
