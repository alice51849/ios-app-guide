What is Aim990: TOEIC Coaching?

Aim990: TOEIC Coaching is an iOS app. Your path to TOEIC success!

Are you ready to conquer the TOEIC and aim for that impressive score of 990? With Aim990, you have a personalized 30-day study coach right at your fingertips. Our app is designed to guide you through daily Listening and Reading study plans, ensuring that you stay on track and focused.

Every learner has unique strengths and weaknesses. That’s why Aim990 includes specialized weak-spot drills to target areas where you need the most improvement. You’ll also find timed practice sessions that simulate real test conditions, helping you build confidence and speed. After each practice, review your mistakes to understand where you went wrong and learn from them.

One of the standout features of Aim990 is our score tracking system. Monitor your progress as you work toward achieving that top TOEIC score. With detailed insights into your performance, you can adjust your study plan to maximize your results.

What’s more? Aim990 offers a one-time unlock option, so you won’t have to worry about ongoing subscriptions. Enjoy an ad-free experience, focused solely on your learning. Your progress is private and stored securely on your device, allowing you to study anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Our user-friendly interface is localized in 50 languages, making it accessible for learners from diverse backgrounds. Dive into your TOEIC preparation with Aim990 and take the first step toward achieving your dream score!

Please note, Aim990 is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with ETS. TOEIC is a trademark of ETS.
