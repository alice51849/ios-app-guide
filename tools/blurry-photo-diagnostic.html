Fast capture fixes for next time

  • Clean the lens before important shots.
  • Tap the subject to lock focus.
  • Use more light instead of heavy digital zoom.
  • Hold for one extra second after tapping shutter.
  • Keep the original file, not only a chat-compressed copy.
