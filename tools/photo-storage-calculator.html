Safe cleanup order

  1. Back up important originals first.
  2. Review huge videos before deleting.
  3. Compare duplicates visually; keep the sharpest original.
  4. Remove screenshots, memes and exports you no longer need.
  5. Empty Recently Deleted only when sure.

Why duplicates matter

Small duplicates add up, but videos usually dominate storage. A good cleanup plan checks both instead of deleting memories blindly.
