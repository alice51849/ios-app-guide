Focus blocking plan

  1. Pick one recurring high-risk block: morning, work session, commute, or bedtime.
  2. Block only the apps that break the block.
  3. Replace the habit with a prepared task: book, workout, notes, or deep work.
  4. Review the saved hours weekly.

What the numbers mean

Small daily habits compound. One hour a day is 365 hours a year: more than nine 40-hour workweeks, or roughly fifteen full 24-hour days.

FAQ

How do I find my iPhone screen time?

Open Settings → Screen Time → See All App & Website Activity, then use daily average or a specific app's average here.

Is all screen time bad?

No. Work, navigation, reading and communication can be valuable. This calculator is best for discretionary apps you want to reduce.

What reduction goal is realistic?

Start with 20–30%. For example, cut a 3-hour habit by 45–60 minutes before attempting a full digital detox.
