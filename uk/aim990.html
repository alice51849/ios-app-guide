What is Aim990 - TOEIC Prep?

Aim990 - TOEIC Prep is an iOS app. Master Your TOEIC Skills

Are you ready to elevate your TOEIC Listening and Reading skills? With Aim990, you have a comprehensive 30-day study coach at your fingertips. Our app is designed to help you tackle the challenges of the TOEIC exam and work towards achieving that coveted score of 990.

Each day, you’ll receive tailored study plans focusing on both Listening and Reading. Our weak-spot drills target the areas where you need the most improvement, ensuring that you make the most of your study time. Plus, timed practice sessions simulate the real exam environment, helping you build your confidence and speed.

Track your progress effortlessly with our intuitive score tracking feature. You’ll see how far you’ve come and what areas still need attention, making your study sessions more effective.

Aim990 is designed with your convenience in mind. Enjoy a one-time unlock option, eliminating the hassle of subscriptions. Our app is completely ad-free, allowing you to focus solely on your studies without distractions. Plus, with no account required, your progress is kept private and stored securely on your device.

Whether you’re at home or on the go, Aim990 works offline, so you can study whenever and wherever you choose. With the interface localized in 50 languages, it’s never been easier to get started on your TOEIC journey.

Please note that Aim990 is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with or endorsed by ETS. TOEIC is a trademark of ETS.
